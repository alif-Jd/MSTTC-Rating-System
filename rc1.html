Delicious Smash Burger

Learn how to make the perfect smash burger at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Butter for toasting buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, ketchup, mustard

Instructions

  • Preheat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  • Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into loose balls.
  • Place the beef balls onto the hot skillet and smash them down with a spatula.
  • Season with salt and pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes per side.
  • Place a slice of cheese on each patty and let it melt.
  • Toast the buns with butter on the skillet.
  • Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
